The typeface chosen to highlight and be at the centre of this project is 'Oddlini'. Released in 2019 by Shuji Kikuchi, Oddlini is a serif font built over Helvetica without being too geometric - rounds and curves of the typeface are not perfect. Combining typography created using the typeface with inspiration drawn from Zodiac signs â€” the project came to be known as Zoddlini. A different take on the everyday deck of cards. Twelve Zodiac symbols replace the traditional face cards. The elements of astrology take place on the four suits.
